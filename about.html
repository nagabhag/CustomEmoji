1. What is the Custom Emoji Maker?

The Custom Emoji Maker is a fun and easy-to-use tool that allows you to design your own emojis. You can choose from a variety of facial features like eyes, nose, mouth, tears, and accessories to create a unique emoji that reflects your personality or mood. Once you’re happy with your design, you can download it as an image and share it with friends, family, or on social media.

2. How to Use the Tool

a. Getting Started

  1. Open the Custom Emoji Maker in your web browser.
  2. You’ll see a circular canvas (the emoji face) on the right and a selection panel on the left.
  3. Use the dropdown menu in the selection panel to choose a category (e.g., eyes, nose, mouth).

b. Adding Features

  1. After selecting a category, you’ll see a list of options (e.g., different types of eyes).
  2. Click on any option to add it to the emoji canvas.
  3. The feature will appear on the canvas, and you can move it around to position it where you like.

c. Moving and Resizing Features

  1. To move a feature, click and drag it to your desired position on the canvas.
  2. To resize a feature, click on it to select it, then use the slider at the top of the screen to adjust its size.

d. Changing the Background Color

  1. Use the color picker tool at the top of the screen to change the background color of the emoji canvas.
  2. Simply click on the color picker and choose a color that you like.

e. Downloading Your Emoji

  1. Once you’re happy with your emoji, click the "Download Emoji" button.
  2. Your emoji will be saved as a PNG image file, which you can share or use however you like.

f. Undo and Reset Options

  1. If you make a mistake, click the "Undo Last" button to remove the last feature you added.
  2. If you want to start over, click the "Reset" button to clear the canvas and begin again.
